Course Overview


In this intensive, hands-on training, you will learn about:


  • Muscle anatomy, phisiology, palpation and testing
  • Muscle Origin, Insertion, Action, and Palpation
  • Active, latent, central and attachment and satellite Myofascial trigger points
  • Myofascial Release
  • Proprioceptive Neuromuscular Facilitation (PNF) Stretching
  • Deep Tissue Techniques


Learning outcomes


Upon successful completion of the Trigger Point Therapy Foundation course, participants will be able to:


  1. Identify and Understand Muscle phisiology and Trigger Points: Recognize the locations and functions of trigger points within various muscle groups and their impact on overall body function.
  2. Demonstrate Palpation Skills: Effectively palpate and evaluate approximately 64 muscles, enhancing assessment and treatment techniques.
  3. Apply Myofascial Release Techniques: Utilize appropriate myofascial release methods to alleviate muscular tension and improve mobility.
  4. Implement PNF Stretching Methods: Integrate Proprioceptive Neuromuscular Facilitation techniques into practice, promoting flexibility and preventing injuries.
  5. Execute Deep Tissue Techniques: Perform deep tissue massage techniques with confidence, addressing client-specific needs and enhancing therapeutic outcomes.
  6. Develop Personalized Treatment Plans: Design and implement tailored treatment plans that incorporate trigger point therapy and associated techniques for various client scenarios.
  7. Evaluate Outcomes: Assess the effectiveness of the treatments provided and make necessary adjustments to enhance client satisfaction and results.
